Traditional Irish Bacon and Cabbage Recipe

Description:

A Bacon and Cabbage Dinner is a quintessential comfort meal, combining crispy bacon with tender, caramelized cabbage for a hearty and satisfying dish.

Rooted in Irish tradition, it offers a delightful balance of smoky flavors and sweet notes, perfect for warming the soul on any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • Smoked Ham
  • Cabbage
  • Salt + Pepper ( to taste )
  • Olive oil or bacon fat (for cooking)
  • Carrots
  • Rooster Potatoes

Steps:

  1. Prepare the bacon by placing it into Hot boiling water.
  2. While the ham is boiling, prepare the cabbage by washing it thoroughly and slicing it into thin strips.
  3. Once the ham is cooked, remove it from the pot and set it aside to cool slightly.
  4. In the same pot of boiling water, add the sliced cabbage and cook until it's tender yet still slightly crisp, usually around 10-15 minutes.
  5. While the cabbage is cooking, slice the ham into bite-sized pieces or strips.
  6. Once the cabbage is cooked to your desired texture, drain it well..
  7. Boil potatoes.
  8. Serve the delicious ham and cabbage Dinner hot, as a hearty and comforting meal that's sure to satisfy. Enjoy!
